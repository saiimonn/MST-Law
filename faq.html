Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our legal services. If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by registering on our platform and using our online scheduling system. Yo may select your preferred attorney, date, and time slot that works best for you.

Do I need to create an account to book an appointment?

Yes, creating an account allows you to manage your appointments, view case updates, and communicate with attorneys.

What types of legal services are offered?

Our firm specializes in various legal fields, including corporate law, family law, criminal defense, real estate, and more. We provide expert legal representation tailored to our clients' needs.

How long does each consultation last?

Consultations may last 30-60 minutes, the duration may depend but the maximum amount of time is 60 minutes. Virtual consultations are also offered.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, security measures are implemented to protect your personal and case-related data.

Who can access my files?

Only you, authorized attorneys, and legal personnel can access your case files.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and online payments.

How can I contact my attorney?

Once your appointment is approved by the attorney, a chat will be provided for you inside the system.
